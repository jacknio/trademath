Challenge Mode Explanation

In Challenge Mode, the difficulty level increases as you progress. There is no limit on the number of questions, and you will be tested to see how many questions you can answer correctly. You will have a time limit of 10 seconds per question. If you answer three questions incorrectly, the challenge will end. At the end of the challenge, you will see your total score and the questions you answered incorrectly.
